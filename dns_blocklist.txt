! =====Blocklist Information=====
! Purpose: To prevent access of websites
! Resource: https://adguard-dns.io/kb/general/dns-filtering-syntax/
! Instructions: Each rule starts with "||" and ends with "^"
! ||example.com^   blocks example.com and all subdomains

! =====Blocklist=====
||youtube.com^

! =====Whitelist Information=====
! Purpose: To allow access to specific websites
! Resource: https://adguard-dns.io/kb/general/dns-filtering-syntax/
! Instructions: Each rule starts with "@@||" and ends with "^"
! @@||example.com^   allows example.com and all subdomains, even if blocked elsewhere

! =====Whitelist=====
@@||music.youtube.com^
